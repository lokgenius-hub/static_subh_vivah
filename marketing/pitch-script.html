🎯 Venue Owner Pitch Script

📋 Yeh Script Kiske Liye Hai?

Jab aap kisi lawn / marriage hall / vatika ke malik se milne jaate hain, tab yeh script use karein. Pehle thoda informal baat karein, phir yeh pitch dein. Koi pressure mat daalein — inka trust banana sabse zaroori hai. Visiting card zaroor dein aur website live dikhaayein phone pe.

🗣️ Step-by-Step Pitch Script
1

Pehla Impression — Apna Parichay Dein

"Namaste ji! Main [Aapka Naam] hun, Bhabua se hi hun. Maine ek website banayi hai — VivahSthal — jo sirf Kaimur district ke wedding venues ke liye hai. Shaadi ke season mein log Google pe search karte hain 'best marriage hall Bhabua' — aur hamaari site unhe seedha aapke pass bhejthi hai."
💡 Tip: Phone nikaalein aur website vivahsthal.in live dikhaayen. Scroll karein, unka venue dikhaayen agar already listed hai.
2

Problem Bataayen — Jo Unhe Hoti Hai

"Ji dekho, aaj kal log JustDial ya Google pe search karte hain venue dhundne ke liye. Agar aapka venue wahan properly listed nahi hai — photos nahi, contact nahi, location nahi — toh customer seedha doosre venue pe chala jaata hai. Aapko pata bhi nahi chalta ki kitne customers miss ho rahe hain har mahine."
3

Solution Dikhaayen — VivahSthal Ka Fayda

"Hamaari site pe aapki ek poori profile hogi — photos, capacity, price range, Google Maps location, aur ek 'Enquiry Karein' button. Jab bhi koi customer form bharta hai — seedha aapke phone pe ya email pe message aayega. Koi middleman nahi. Lead directly aapko."
4

Offer Dikhaayen — FREE Listing

"Aur suniye — pehle 6 mahine bilkul FREE hai. Ek bhi rupaya nahi lagega. Humara kaam hai ki pehle aapko customers dilaayen, phir payment ki baat karein. Agar ek bhi booking aati hai hamaari site se, toh aap khud sochein ki yeh useful hai ya nahi."
💡 Tip: Yahan ruko. Unhe react karne do. Aksar yahan woh "haan" bol dete hain.
5

Closing — Ek Simple Commitment Maangein

"Bas ek kaam karein — mujhe apne venue ki 4-5 photos WhatsApp kar dijiye aur apna phone number de dijiye. Main aapki poori profile free mein bana doonga — photos, description, location sab. Aapko kuch nahi karna sirf ek baar dekh lena ki profile theek lagi ya nahi. Deal hai?"
💡 Tip: Abhi wahan se unka number lein aur WhatsApp pe apna naam save karaayein. Visiting card dein. Photo shoot offer kar sakte hain pehle visit ke liye.

🤔 Common Objections — Aur Jawab

❓ "Internet pe kya hoga, hamare yahan toh sab jaante hain hume."
"Ji bilkul, local log toh jaante hain. Lekin shaadi ke liye Patna, Varanasi, Ara se bhi log dhundthe hain — jo aapko nahi jaante. Woh log Google pe search karte hain. Aur nayi generation ke bachche bhi pehle online check karte hain phir jaate hain — to aapke existing customers ke bache bhi."
❓ "Free mein hai toh baad mein kitna paisa loge?"
"Ji, 6 mahine ke baad ek basic subscription hoga — around ₹499–₹999 per month jo ki ek shaadi booking se asaani se recover ho jaata hai. Aur jo venues aag-le 6 mahine se hain unhe discount milega. Abhi toh sirf try karo — kucch kho nahi rahe."
❓ "Hamari photos nahi hain achhi."
"Koi baat nahi — main khud aakar kuch photos le lunga pehli baar. Ya aap jo bhi WhatsApp pe bhejenge woh kaam kar lenge. Profile baad mein update bhi ho sakti hai jab bhi aapke paas better photos hon."
❓ "Sochke batate hain / baad mein aana."
"Bilkul ji, koi problem nahi. Main aapko 2 din baad ek WhatsApp message karoonga — ek demo link bhi bhejoonga jisme main ek sample profile banaata hun aapke venue ka, aap dekhna phir decide karna. Kucch nahi jaata try karne mein."

🎯 Yaad Rakho — Ek Baat

Aapka goal pehle visit mein unka phone number aur 1 photo lena hai.
Booking confirm karna ya payment lena abhi zaroorat nahi.

Trust pehle, business baad mein.
Ek khush venue owner 5 aur venues refer kar deta hai — apne aap.
